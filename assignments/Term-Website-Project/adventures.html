SURROUND

Length: 12 Miles

Skill: Route biker

Cost: $ 40.00

Location: Around Mountain Peak


Surrounding Mountain Peak is a wonderful adventure, very well enjoyed by teams and groups of friends. Willing to have a great time outdoors during any time of the year. Having the chance to meet Peak River and take a rest at its shore.
The adventure includes hidration and full support and repair on the way.
Come with your pals and have a great time !!

CLIMB

Length: 5 Miles Upwards

Skill: Mountain biker & strong conviction

Cost: $ 70.00

Location: Up Greeny Mountain


Greeny Mountain has become the allways current challenge for champion mountain bikers of all the region. It's beautiful colored sides and amazing landscapes make it a great trip for those who want to test themselves and conquer the Giant.
We will be side by side to support you on the journey up. Were you will enjoy the preffered outlook of the area.
The adventure will include hidration and full repair on the way, as well as our power shot breakfast.
